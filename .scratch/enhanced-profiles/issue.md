## What to build
Enhance the chicken records with a chronological notes log and a photo gallery. Users should be able to add, edit, and delete notes (e.g., vet visits). The photo gallery should display all photos for a chicken in chronological order.

## Acceptance criteria
- [ ] Each chicken record has a chronological log of notes.
- [ ] Users can add, edit, and delete notes.
- [ ] A gallery displays all photos for a chicken with their descriptions and timestamps.
